Executive Summary

Game Concept:

    
 
     First-person shooting may be structurally composed of levels Others different environments 
     which are not divided into levels and can be explored freely.   
     Games feature multiple difficulty settings; in harder modes, enemies are tougher, 
     more aggressive and do more damage, and power-ups are limited. In easier modes, the player can succeed 
     through reaction times alone on more difficult settings.

Genre:

 As the name indicates shooting game so it lies in Action category.

Target audience:

 As per Shooting games our target audience will be adults

Project Scope

    

    
    The game will be structure like that where one player will shoot or kill enemies to move on to next stage. 
    As the player move on to next stages the environment will also changes, it will be tough for the player to 
    tackle with enemies in different environments. But the player will be able to use different weapons and grenades,
    In our game where in the player may move up and down and left and right around the screen, typically firing straight forward.
    Diffferent weapons, and can be used in combination with other tools such as grenades for indirect offense, armor for 
    additional defense, or may be we use accessories such as telescope sights to modify the behavior of the weapons.
